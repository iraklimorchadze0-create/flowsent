The problem

Referrals aren't a pipeline

Word of mouth got you here. But referrals arrive on their schedule, not yours — and every month starts with the same question: where does the next deal come from?

Hiring an SDR is a salary plus months of ramp. Paid ads are a slow, expensive experiment. Cold email is still the most direct line to your exact buyer — when it's done right. Done badly, it burns your domain and gets ignored.

How it works

Cold email fails in the plumbing,
not the pitch

So plumbing is where we start. Four steps, in order:

  1. 1

    Step 1: Infrastructure first

    We buy secondary sending domains and warm them properly before anything sends. Your main domain never sends a cold email — its reputation is never at risk.

  2. 2

    Step 2: Targeting and lists

    We define your ideal customer together, then build the list with proprietary data enrichment and verify every address. Bad data is the fastest way to burn a sending domain — so we don't buy databases.

  3. 3

    Step 3: Copy that earns replies

    We write the campaigns, and our AI researches each prospect individually — so every email reads like it was written for one person, because it was.

  4. 4

    Step 4: Replies become calls

    We manage the inbox, qualify interested replies, and book them straight onto your calendar. You show up and sell.

FAQ

Questions, answered

Will this put my domain at risk?

No. Everything sends from secondary domains we buy, warm, and monitor separately. Your main domain is never used for outreach — that's the first thing we set up, before any email goes out.

How soon do calls start landing?

Domain warmup comes first — rushing it is how campaigns end up in spam. Once sending starts, the target is simple: 10–15 qualified demos on your calendar in your first 30 days.

Is this just AI spam?

No. Our AI researches each prospect individually — their company, their role, what they're working on — and writes from that research. If a line reads like a template, it doesn't go out.

What makes your emails different from the ones I delete?

The ones you delete came from a template and went to ten thousand people. Ours go out in controlled volumes from warmed domains, and every message is built from research on that specific prospect — their company, their role, their timing. Relevance is the entire strategy.

What if cold email isn't right for my business?

Then you'll hear it from us on the strategy call, before you've spent anything. Cold email works when the offer is strong and the buyers are reachable — if that's not your situation, we'll tell you straight.
