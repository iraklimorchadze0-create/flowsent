The problem

Referrals aren't a pipeline

Agencies live on word of mouth. SaaS founders live on their network. Both run out — and every month starts with the same question: where does the next deal come from?

Hiring an SDR is a salary plus months of ramp. Ads for your own pipeline are a slow, expensive experiment. Cold email is still the most direct line to your exact buyer — when it's done right. Done badly, it burns your domain and gets ignored.

How it works

Cold email fails in the plumbing,
not the pitch

So plumbing is where we start. Four steps, in order:

  1. 1

    Step 1: Infrastructure first

    We buy secondary sending domains, configure SPF, DKIM, and DMARC, and warm the inboxes for two to three weeks. Your main domain never sends a cold email.

  2. 2

    Step 2: Targeting and lists

    We define your ideal customer together, then hand-build and verify the list. Bad data is the fastest way to burn a sending domain — so we don't buy databases.

  3. 3

    Step 3: Copy that earns replies

    We write the campaigns and personalize at scale with AI — every line human-reviewed. You approve everything before launch.

  4. 4

    Step 4: Replies become calls

    We manage the inbox, qualify interested replies, and book them onto your calendar. First campaigns typically go live around week three.

FAQ

Fair questions

Will this put my domain at risk?

No. Everything sends from secondary domains we buy, warm, and monitor separately. Your main domain is never used for outreach — that's the first thing we set up, before any email goes out.

How soon do calls start landing?

Setup and warmup take the first two to three weeks; campaigns go live around week three. We won't promise a number of calls — anyone who does is guessing. What we can promise is that you'll see exactly what's working, and what we're changing when something isn't.

Is this just AI spam?

No. We define your ICP together, hand-build the list, and verify every address. AI drafts the personalization; a human reviews every line before it sends. If a line reads like a template, it doesn't go out.

Do you have case studies?

Not yet — we're new, and we'd rather say that than fake it. Book the call and we'll walk you through exactly what we'd build for you instead.

What do you need from me?

About two hours up front: one call to define your ICP and offer, plus sign-off on the lead list and copy. Then a calendar link. We handle the rest, including replies.

What does it cost?

It depends on scope — a single campaign is different from an always-on outbound engine. You'll get a straight number on the strategy call, not a surprise line item later.
